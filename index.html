About Me

My name is Niko Viitiö and i'm currently 23 years old. I'm currently the CEO of Normogames. I'm studying at XAMK Kotka in game programming. I have graduated from vocational school as car mechanic in 2018 but i have always found games and programming interesting. From now on i have programmed & modelled for few years or so.

Game Development Experience

My first touch to game programming came when i was a child and i wanted to mod Terraria. I somehow managed to make some simple mods but after all my interest came to an end. When i grow up i purchased Game Maker: Studio and i made some simple 2D games with that and again my interest ended. In 2018 i started learning Unity on my free time and since then i have spent most of my free time in making games with Unity.


I have made few mobile games that can be downloaded on Google Play, they aren't so complex but making them has taught me a lot of game development and programming. They are basically endless runners with pixel art graphics and different themes. You can find out more of them on their own project pages.
