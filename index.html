About Me

My name is Niko Viitiö and i'm currently 23 years old and i'm the CEO of Normogames. I'm studying game programming at XAMK Kotka. I have graduated from vocational school as car mechanic in 2018 but i have always found games and programming interesting. From now on i have programmed & modelled for few years or so and published few mobile games on Google Play.

Game Development Experience

I started my programming when i was child. I played a lot game called "Terraria" and i wanted to develop my own mods to that game. Now years has passed on and i have used Unity and Blender to develop games that i like. Currenty i'm working with a multiplayer FPS game called "The Riflemen".


I have made few mobile games that can be downloaded on Google Play, they aren't so complex but making them has taught me a lot about game development and programming. They are basically endless runners with pixel art graphics and different themes. You can find them on Normogames' site on Google Play.
