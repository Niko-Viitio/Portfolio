The Riflemen

Multiplayer FPS made in Unity using Photon

What's That?

Started in july 2021 as an one man project, The Riflemen is multiplayer FPS made in Unity and it uses Photon Pun 2 for networking. Games setting is loosely based on World war 2 and graphics are low poly. Gameplay is mix between old arena shooters and modern FPS games. Players have only bolt action rifle and combat knife, how ever it's possible to order supply drops that rewards players with more powerful weapons like shotgun, sniper rifle or bazooka.


Game currently has 8 maps, 5 weapons & 2 gamemodes (team deathmatch and domination). Players can customize their weapons look freely using 16 different materials. How ever they need to level up their character to unlock these skins.
